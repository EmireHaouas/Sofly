ROBIN CAP 10

BEFORE START

  • PARKING BRAKE ON
  • MASTER SWITCH
  • ALTERNATOR SWITCH OFF
  • MAGNETOS OFF
  • ELECTRICAL EQUIPMENT OFF
  • AVIONICS SWITCH ON
  • MIXTURE LEAN

ENGINE START

  • MASTER SWITCH ON
    COLD ENGINE
  • FLAPS RETRACTED - CHECKED
  • STROBE LIGHTS ON
  • THROTTLE FULL
  • MIXTURE FULL RICH
  • TANK SELECTOR SWITCH AVANT
  • ELECTRIC PUMP ON
  • FUEL FLOW METER CHECKED
  • ELECTRIC PUMP OFF
  • THROTTLE 1 CM
  • MIXTURE LEAN
  • MAGNETOS BOTH
  • STARTER PRESS (DEMARREUR)
  • MIXTURE RICH WHEN ENGINE FIRES
  • THROTTLE 1000 RPM
    HOT ENGINE
  • MASTER SWITCH ON
  • STROBES LIGHT ON
  • THROTTLE 4 TO 5 CM
  • MIXTURE LEAN
  • MAGNETOS BOTH
  • STARTER PRESS (DEMARREUR)
  • MIXTURE RICH WHEN ENGINE FIRES
  • THROTTLE 1000 RPM

BEFORE TAXI

  • ENGINE SPEED 1000 TO 2000 RPM
  • OIL PRESSURE OIL PRESSURE
  • CUT-OFF TEST (MAGNETOS) DONE
  • ALTERNATOR SWITCH ON
  • BATTERY CHARGE CHECKED
  • HORIZON ON
  • DIRECTIONAL ON
  • POSITIONS LIGHTS ON
  • ALTIMETER SET
  • TRANSPONDER STBY
  • REAR TANK TEST 5 MIN
    (IF USE INTENDED)

TAXI

  • PARKING BRAKE RELEASE
  • BRAKES AND STEERING TEST DONE

RUN-UP

  • PEDAL BRAKES APPLIED
  • TEMPS AND PRESSURES CHECKED
  • MIXTURE FULL RICH
  • THROTTLE 1800RPM
  • MAGNETOS L, BOTH, R, BOTH
    MAX DROP 125 RPM; MAX DIFF 50 RPM
  • THROTTLE IDLE
  • THROTTLE 1000 RPM

BEFORE TAKE OFF

  • TANK SELECTOR SWITCH FWD
  • MIXTURE FULL RICH
  • MASTER SWITCH ON
  • ALTERNATOR SWITCH ON
  • BATTERY CHARGE CHECKED
  • MAGNETOS BOTH
  • FLIGHT CONTROLS FREE & CORRECT
  • ELEVATOR TAB CHECKED; NEUTRAL
  • FLAPS T/O
  • MECH ACCELEROMETER RESET
  • CANOPY CLOSED AND LOCKED
  • BRAKES RELEASED
  • LANDING LIGHT ON
  • TRANSPONDER ALT
