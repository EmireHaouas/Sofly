AVIAT PITTS SPECIAL S-2S

Before start

  • FUEL QUALITY CHECKED
  • BRAKES SET
  • ALL ELECTRIC OFF
  • AVIONICS MASTER OFF

ENGINE START

  • FUEL VALVE ON
    (TOP LEFT POSITION)
  • COWL FLAP OPEN
  • PROP LEVER FULL FORWARD
  • MIXTURE LEVER FULL RICH
  • THROTTLE FULL OPEN
  • BATTERY ON
    (THIRD SWITCH)
  • BOOST PUMP ON 4 SECONS
  • THROTTLE 1/2 INCH OPEN
  • MIXTURE IDLE CUT-OFF
  • AREA CLEAR
  • STARTER KEY ENGAGE
  • MIXTURE FULL FORWARD
    (WHEN ENGINE FIRES)

AFTER START

  • THROTTLE 1000 RPM
  • OIL PRESSURE CHECK
  • ALTERNATOR FIELD SWITCH ON & CHECK
  • AVIONICS SWITCH ON

BEFORE TAKEOFF

  • BRAKERS SET
  • TAIL WHEEL STRAIGHT
  • OIL TEMP GREEN
  • COWL FLAP OPEN
  • MIXTURE FULL RICH
  • THROTTLE 1700 RPM
  • OIL PRESSURE & TEMP GREEN
  • AMMETER CHECKED
  • PROP CYCLE
  • THROTTLE 2200 RPM
  • MAGS LEFT, BOTH, RRIGHT BOTH
    (MAX DROP 50 RPM; MAX DIFF 175 RPM)
  • THROTTLE IDLE
  • THROTTLE 1000 RPM
  • FLAT CONTROLS FREE & CORRECT
  • ELEVATOR TRIM NEUTRAL
    (HANDLE ON THE LEFT HORIZONTAL)
  • FUEL SELECTOR CHECK ON
  • BOOST PUMP ON
  • BRAKES RELEASE

TAKE OFF

  • THROTTLE FULL
  • 70 mpg ROTATE
  • CLIMB 90MPH

AFTER TAKE OFF

  • PROP LEVER AS RQRD
  • FUEL PUMP OFF

LANDING

  • BOOST PUMP ON
  • COWL FLAP CLOSED
  • MIXTURE SET
  • PROP LEVER FULL FORWARD

LANDING

  • BOOST PUMP ON
  • COWL FLAPH CLOSED
  • MIXTURE SET
  • PROP LEVER FULL FORWARD

AFTER LANDING

  • COWL FLAP OPEN
  • BOOST PUMP OFF
  • TRANSPONDER STBY

PARKING

  • THROTTLE 1000 RPM
  • AVIONICS MASTER OFF
  • ALTERNATOR FIELD OFF
  • MAGNETOR GROUP CHECK
  • MIXTURE IDLE CUT OFF
  • IGNITION KEY OFF
  • MASTER SWITCH OFF
