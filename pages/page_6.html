  • THROTTLE 1/4 OPEN
  • FUEL PUMP SWITCH ON

  • OIL PRESSURE BOTH
  • FUEL PUMP SWITCH OFF
  • FUEL PUMP SWITCH ON

TAXI

  • BRAKES RELEASE
  • BRAKES CHECK
  • FLIGHT INSTRUMENTS CHECK

BEFORE TAKEOFF

  • MIXTURE CHECK FULL RICH

  • ENGINE INSTRUMENTS ON

  • 59 KIAS ROTATE
  • CLIMB 87 KIAS

CLIMB

  • FUEL PUMP SWITCH OFF
  • SPEED 104 KIAS
  • MIXTURE AS RQRD

CRUISE

  • THROTTLE 2100 - 2700 RPM
  • MIXTURE LEAN 1 INCH

LANDING

  • MIXTURE FULL RICH
  • FUEL PUMP SWITCH ON

AFTER LANDING

  • FUEWL PUMP SWITCH OFF
  • TRIM NEUTRAL

PARKING

  • THROTTLE CLOSED
  • MIXTURE IDLE CUTOFF
  • BATTERY MASTER SWITCH OFF
