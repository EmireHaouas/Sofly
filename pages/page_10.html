BOEING F/A-18E/F

Before start

  • APU SWITCH OFF
  • LDG/TAXI LIGHT SWITCH OFF
  • THROTTLES OFF
  • EXTR LTS MSTR SWITCH FORWARD
    (SIDE OF THROTTLE)
  • PARKING BRAKE SET
  • ANTI-SKID SWITCH ON
  • FLAPS SWITCH AUTO
  • LANDING GEAR LEVER DOWN
  • L(R)DDI,HUD,UFCD&MCPD KNOBS OFF
  • VOLUME KNOBS OFF
  • HOOK LEVER UP, LIGHT OUT
  • WINGFOLD SWITCH CHECK
    MATCHING ACTUAL WING POSITION
  • GENERATOR SWITCHES NORM
  • BATTERY SWITCH OFF
  • INT LIGHT SWITCHES AS RQRD

ENGINE START

  • BATTERY SWITCH ON
  • APU SWITCH ON
  • APU READY LIGHT CHECK ON
    WHEN RIGHT RPM HITS 10%
  • ENGINE CRANK SWITCH R
    WHEN RIGHT RPM HITS 10%
  • RIGHT THROTTLE IDLE
  • RIGHT OIL PRESS CHECK MIN 10 PSI
    (WITHIN 30 SECS)
  • RIGHT EGT CHECK 871 ̊C MAX
  • L(R) DDI, HUD & MCPD KNOBS ON
  • BLEED AIR KNOB. NORM
  • ENGINE CRANK SWITCH L
    WHEN LEFT RPM HITS 10%
  • LEFT THROTTLE IDLE
  • LEFT OIL PRESS CHECK MIN 10 PSI
    (WITHIN 30 SECS)
  • LEFT EGT. CHECK 871 ̊C MAX
  • ENG CRANK SWITCH CHECK OFF

BEFORE TAXI

  • UFCD KNOB ON
  • WINGFOLD SWITCH SPREAD
  • FLIGHT CONTROLS FREE & CORRECT
  • TRIM 4 ̊ NU
  • ALTIMETER SET
  • FLAPS SWITCH HALF
  • APU CHECK OFF
  • NOSE WHEEL STEERING ENGAGE
    (BUTTON BACKSIDE OF STICK)
  • LDG/TAXI LIGHT ON
  • PARKING BRAKE RELEASE

BEFORE TAKE OFF

  • FLIGHT CONTROLS FREE & CORRECT
  • WINGS CHECK SPREAD & LOCKED
  • FLAPS CHECK HALF
  • HOOK CHECK UP, LIGHT OUT

TAKE OFF

  • THROTTLES MIL (MAX IF RQRD)
  • ±165 KIAS ROTATE
  • LANDING GEAR UP
  • FLAPS AUTO

AFTER TAKE OFF

  • LANDING GEAR CHECK UP
  • FLAPS CHECK AUTO
  • LDG/TAXI LIGHT OFF

Not to be used for real-world navigation or aviation purposes.
